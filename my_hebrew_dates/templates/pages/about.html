About Us

My Hebrew Dates is a platform designed to make it simple and convenient for families to create personalized Hebrew event calendars. We understand the importance of keeping track of Hebrew dates, such as birthdays and anniversaries, and we want to help you celebrate these special occasions with your loved ones.

Our goal is to provide an intuitive and user-friendly interface that allows you to easily create custom calendars and share them with your family members. With our service, you can generate corresponding English event labels for a better understanding of the dates, ensuring everyone stays connected and informed.

About the Developer

Abe Hanoka is the developer behind My Hebrew Dates. With a passion for both programming and Hebrew culture, Abe envisioned a platform that would bridge the gap between Hebrew dates and modern technology.

Abe's expertise in web development and dedication to creating innovative solutions led to the creation of My Hebrew Dates. By combining his technical skills with a deep understanding of Hebrew traditions, Abe has crafted a tool that simplifies the process of managing Hebrew event calendars.
