Welcome to MyHebrewDates!

Never miss an important Hebrew date again. MyHebrewDates is a web application that allows you to save and manage important Hebrew dates, such as birthdays, anniversaries, and yartzeits.

The webapp provides users with an iCal file containing all of their saved dates, which they can subscribe to into their preferred calendar application.

Please note that this application is currently under development and is not yet available to the public.
